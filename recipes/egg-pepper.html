Egg Pepper

Description:

This is a recipe of a delicious egg cooked sunny side up with egg pepper. A energetic and big start to your day!

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Bell peppers
  • Sesame oil (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cut bell peppers
  2. Let it cook in pan until satisfied
  3. Crack eggs in pan
  4. Put the lid on the pan until egg yolk is cooked
